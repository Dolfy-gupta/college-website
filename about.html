About Us

We are the world's largest university

Lovely Professional University (LPU) is a Private University situated on the outskirts of Jalandhar, Punjab. The university is approved by University Grants Commission (UGC) and is a member of the Association of Indian Universities (AIU). It is the largest single-campus university in India, with more than 30,000 students, and over 4000 faculties and staff, offering 300+ programs across its 45 departments. LPU is also ranked 23rd among the Top Universities of India and is ranked 2nd in Business and Economics, 8th in Clinical and Health, 9th in Computer Science, and 6th in Engineering & Life Sciences in all of India as per the Times Higher Education World University Rankings 2023. LPU ranked 47th in NIRF Universities Rankings 2022.
