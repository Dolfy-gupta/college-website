Lovely Professional University is recognized by University Grants Commission (UGC), a statutory body of the Government of India for the coordination, determination and maintenance of standards of university education in India, under section 2(f) of the UGC Act, 1956.


Teacher education programmes B.Ed., M.Ed., B.Ed. - M.Ed. (Integrated), B.A. B.Ed/B.sc. B.Ed. ; and Physical Education programmes B.P.Ed. and M.P.Ed. are recognized by National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE), a statutory body of the Government of India for teacher education system.

Pharmacy and M.Pharmacy programmes are approved by Pharmacy Council of India (PCI), a statutory body of the Government of India for the regulation of the Pharmacy Education for the purpose of registration as a pharmacist under the Pharmacy Act and for regulation of Profession and Practice of Pharmacy.


3 years LL.B programme and 5 years integrated B.A.LL.B (Hons.), BBA LL.B (Hons.) programmes are recognized by Bar Council of India (BCI), a statutory body of the Government of India to regulate and represent the Indian Bar.


Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) is recognized by the Indian Association of Physiotherapists, a non-governmental organization that represents the physiotherapy profession at the National and International Levels. The IAP is registered under the Indian society act, in the year 1955 and member of the world confederation of physical therapy. After the BPT degree, the students are eligible to get a membership and practice anywhere in India.
